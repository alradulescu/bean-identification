How it works

Follow the steps below to get a personalised V60 pour-over recipe for your coffee. All analysis runs locally on this server – no internet connection or API key required.

  1. Label – take or upload a photo of the bag label.
  2. Beans – photograph the whole beans on a white background.
  3. Grounds (optional) – photograph your ground coffee for extra precision.
  4. Recipe – view your personalised brew recipe.
  5. Feedback – rate the cup to refine the recipe for next time.

Step 1 of 5 Scan the Coffee Bag Label
📷 What to do

Take a photo or upload an image of the printed label on your coffee bag.

  • Make sure the label text is clearly visible and in focus.
  • Ensure the image is well-lit to help the text reader extract information accurately.
  • The app will extract: origin, roast level, altitude (MASL), process, tasting notes, coffee variety (e.g. Geisha, Caturra), and decaf/half-caf status.
Accepted formats: JPG, PNG, WEBP. On mobile, you can take a photo directly.

Step 2 of 5 Photograph the Whole Beans
📷 What to do

Place a small handful of whole, unground beans on a plain white surface (e.g. a sheet of A4 paper) and take a photo.

  • Spread the beans so they are not overlapping each other.
  • Use good overhead lighting to avoid shadows.
  • The app will analyse: bean colour, size, uniformity, and density estimate.
⚠️ Please complete Step 1 first – the app needs label information before analysing beans.
Accepted formats: JPG, PNG, WEBP. On mobile, you can take a photo directly.

✓ Done Bean Analysis

You can now photograph your ground coffee for extra recipe precision, or skip straight to the recipe.

Step 3 of 5 · Optional Photograph the Ground Coffee
📷 What to do Optional

Grind a small amount of coffee and spread it thinly on a white surface. Take a close-up photo.

  • This step is optional but improves recipe accuracy significantly.
  • Ensure the grounds are spread in a thin, even layer.
  • The app will estimate: particle size distribution, fines percentage, and grind uniformity.
  • If you skip this step, the recipe will be generated from the label and bean data alone.
Accepted formats: JPG, PNG, WEBP. On mobile, you can take a photo directly.

Step 5 of 5 Rate Your Brew
✍️ What to do

After brewing and tasting your cup, rate each characteristic below. The app will adjust the recipe for your next brew based on your feedback.

  • Extraction assessment: choose how balanced the cup tasted overall.
  • Sliders: rate acidity, sweetness, bitterness, and body from 1 (low) to 5 (high).
  • After submitting, an updated recipe will appear below the form.
⚠️ Please complete Steps 1–2 first before submitting feedback.
